QuickShift For Chrome

Guidelines:
Turn on or off features using the checkboxes. To customize a key combination, click in the appropriate box and type your desired hotkey. Not all keys are allowed. (Including all the built-in Chrome keyboard shortcut keys.) Also be aware of potential conflicts with other exenstions you have installed and with any global hotkeys set by other applications. One final note: Although you are allowed to use unmodified keys (e.g. "K" or "F11"), I highly recommend modifying all your choices with Control, Shift, Alt or some combination of the three.
